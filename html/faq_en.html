How the SafeNote privacy works?

SafeNote integrates various methods to take care of the privacy of users'notes:

  • Access control with password lock in case of multiple login errors.
  • Cyphering key, in addition to the password, to reactivate 10 minutes after the latest user interaction.
  • Automatic close of idle open note after 5 minutes.
  • SSL communication between users devices and AuverCloud servers.

Can I change my password?

Yes, at any time, either by resetting the account from the application home screen or by changing the password from the preferences screen.

Can I change my cyphering key?

Not yet. When defined, the cyphering cannot be changed. If you want to use a new cyphering key, just create a new account with the required key.

Is there any limitation in terms of number or size of notes?

No.

How to be sure of what SafeNote does?

The SafeNote code is open sourced on GitHub.
